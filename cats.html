# Octocat Classifier :octopus: :cat: :mag:
\n\n              
![](https://img.shields.io/badge/build-passing-brightgreen) ![](https://img.shields.io/badge/coverage-90%25-green) ![](https://img.shields.io/badge/dependencies-up%20to%20date-brightgreen)
\n\n              As the name suggests, Octocat Classifier is used to determine whether a given image contains an Octocat. It is trained with images from the 
[Octodex](1)
, images shared with 
[#MyOctocat on Twitter](2)
, and 
[photographs of laptops with :octocat: stickers on them]()
.\n\n
